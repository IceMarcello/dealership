About us

Being a friend of a car dealership in Lodz, I noticed that the entire system of management, cost calculation and information about cars and their current condition is based on the Microsoft Excel application. After researching the problems of using a spreadsheet to manage such a business, I found this solution to be problematic in many ways. The problems start as soon as the program is turned on. Storing a large amount of data in spreadsheets causes applications to run for a very long time. In addition, employees do not store photos in it. To find a car, they use the VIN number, even with a small number of currently available cars. This is quite inconvenient for the employees themselves. Most costs are calculated in a separate spreadsheet, which requires a second spreadsheet window to be launched, which slows down computers even more. The calculations themselves are not stored anywhere, and often, if necessary, are written down on cards. I also noticed that employees use the OTOMOTO website to post ads. Combining these two facts, designing a website to manage such a salon with the possibility of listing offers for customers on your own website brings several benefits.
